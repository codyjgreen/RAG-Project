# Acme Corp — Remote Work Policy

*Effective: January 2026. Applies to all full-time Acme Corp employees in the United States.*

## 1. Eligibility
Employees become eligible for remote work after completing their **90-day probationary period**. Once eligible, an employee may work remotely up to **three (3) days per week** with manager approval. **Fully remote** arrangements (4–5 days per week) require additional sign-off from a **Vice President** or above.

## 2. Core Hours
Regardless of location, all employees must be reachable during **core hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM** in their local time zone. Work outside core hours is flexible as long as weekly responsibilities are met.

## 3. Equipment and Stipends
Acme provides every remote-eligible employee with a **company laptop**. In addition:
- A **one-time home-office stipend of $500** is available to set up a workspace.
- A **monthly internet reimbursement of $50** is provided to employees working remotely at least two days per week.

Stipends are claimed through the HR portal and require an itemized receipt.

## 4. Security Requirements
- Employees must connect through the **company VPN** when accessing internal systems.
- **Public Wi-Fi must not be used** for handling confidential or customer data.
- Laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled (configured by IT before issue).

## 5. Requesting Remote Work
Submit a remote-work request through the **HR portal at least two (2) weeks in advance**. Your direct manager is the approver for arrangements of up to three days per week. Requests are reviewed within five business days.

## 6. Revocation
Remote-work privileges may be paused or revoked if performance expectations are not met or if security requirements are violated. Employees will receive written notice and a two-week transition period where practical.
