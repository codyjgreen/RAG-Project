NIMBUS NOTES — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
(Fictional product. For RAG testing only.)

PLANS AND PRICING
Q: Is there a free plan?
A: Yes. The Free plan includes 1 GB of storage and syncing across up to 3 devices.

Q: How much does Nimbus Pro cost?
A: Nimbus Pro is $8 per month, billed monthly. It includes unlimited storage and syncing across up to 10 devices.

Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes. You can cancel at any time from Settings > Billing. If you cancel within 14 days of a payment, you are eligible for a full refund.

FEATURES
Q: What formats can I export my notes to?
A: Notes can be exported as Markdown, PDF, or HTML.

Q: Does Nimbus work offline?
A: Offline mode is available on the Pro plan only. Free-plan notes require an internet connection to open.

Q: Can I use Nimbus on my phone and laptop at the same time?
A: Yes. Notes sync automatically across all your registered devices, up to your plan's device limit.

DATA AND SECURITY
Q: Is my data encrypted?
A: All notes are encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Data is stored in the United States by default. Pro users can opt into EU data residency from Settings > Privacy.

SUPPORT
Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@nimbus.example. We respond within 24 hours on business days.

Q: Do you offer phone support?
A: Not at this time. Support is handled by email only.
